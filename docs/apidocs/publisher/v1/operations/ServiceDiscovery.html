Service Discovery

Get list of services in the cluster.

GET https://apis.wso2.com/api/am/publisher/v1/service-discovery/endpoints/{type}

Using this operation, you can retrieve complete list of available services in the clusters.

OAuth 2.0 Scope

Not required

Examples

required
